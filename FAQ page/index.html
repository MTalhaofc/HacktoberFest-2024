Changing an email address can be complicated due to platform restrictions, security protocols, or identity verification requirements. Many services use emails as primary identifiers, making it difficult to switch without losing access or disrupting important communications.

To contact support, visit the service's official website or app and navigate to the "Help" or "Contact Us" section. Many platforms offer options like live chat, email, or submitting a ticket through their support portal.
